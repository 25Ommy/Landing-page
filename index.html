About us
In our African culture pots are made out of clay and are designed to create the best flavours on the natural fire. We grew up eating various types of our African cuisine from the African pot. Some of the best recipes have disappeared with time and the skills to create them have gone with our elders.
African Cuisine Restaurant aims to recreate the taste as it used to be with special attention to detail. Our African dish is based on Bogobe (also known as Pap) and everything else is designed to accompany it. Bogobe in Sepedi is created from various types of millet: chiefly maize and maize meal(coarsely ground maze).
Various types of relish are often served along with Bogobe. This relish ranges from beef stew, ox trotters, tripe with offals, free range chicken and the popular Mopane worms mostly found in the forests of Venda in South Africa. Green vegetables are also a supplement in every plate, they come either fried or in peanut butter. We believe this concept of African Traditional food must be shared among all food lovers internationally. It does not need to be a secret. So come along and experience a unique African cuisine dining experience at Ohms African Cuisine Restaurant.
